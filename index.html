The flow

From raw air to clear action in 3 steps

STEP 01

Share your location or search a city

One tap geolocation, or type any city name worldwide using live search.

STEP 02

AirVision fetches real-time air data

Free open sensor data returns AQI plus PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO₂, SO₂ and Ozone instantly.

STEP 03

Claude AI explains the risk & gives tips

Plain-language health risk, outdoor activity advice, and one practical action for today.

UN Sustainable Development Goal 13

Climate Action

SDG 13 calls for urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts — strengthening resilience, improving education and awareness, and integrating climate measures into everyday decisions. AirVision was built as a practical, hands-on response: it turns invisible air pollution and carbon emissions into numbers people can see, understand, and act on.
